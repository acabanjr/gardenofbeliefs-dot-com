The Garden of Beliefs

A heartwarming tale of friendship, understanding, and celebrating differences.

Daisy the Daffodil embarks on a journey through a magical garden where every flower represents a unique belief. From creativity to optimism, each flower claims their belief is best, leading to division in the once harmonious garden. With the gentle wisdom of two ancient trees, Daisy helps the flowers discover the power of acceptance and unity, and the garden blooms brighter than ever before.

Tommy Tulip

A Tulip who believes in creativity. He spends his days painting vibrant colors and stunning landscapes.

Rebecca Rose

A Rose who cherishes love and compassion. Always available to listen attentively and offer comfort to their fellow flowers.

Stacy Sunflower

A Sunflower who values optimism. Her cheerful demeanor brightens the garden, and she spreads positivity wherever she goes.

Daisy Daffodil

A Daffodil who believes in harmony and peace. She thinks her perspective is the key to maintaining balance in the garden.

In this garden, all sorts of flowers flourish, each representing the beliefs and ideas of the village. It is a colorful and diverse place.

Wise Willow Willy

An ancient smile of tales untold and wisdom's gold, Wise Willow Willy smiled, with a twinkle in their eye, Well defined, timeless in heart and of mind.

Kind Oak Oatley

With branches like arms, and leaves like whispers, gently asway, sharing kindness and wisdom, both by night and by day. With roots that run deep, they have been grounded for long, a symbol of life standing tall, upright, and strong.

About the Author

Andrés Cabán Jr.

"As a kid, I had a book full of 'My Inventions,' a binder divided into various categories. It was full of my ideas. Detailed mock-ups ranging from clothing designs to video game concepts, and even blueprints for skyscrapers. I've always been creative and even more so, organized. These adjectives define my character and guide my approach to even the smallest details."

Andrés has always been drawn to creative pursuits. His combination of imagination and meticulous organization has shaped both his career and personal journeys. His passion for writing began in his teenage years, fueled by poetry and a love for essays. Though writing a book has been a long-held goal, it wasn’t until recently that the idea for a children’s book took root. After careful thought, especially regarding the illustrations, Andrés is proud to share his debut children’s book, The Garden of Beliefs, blending his love for storytelling with visual art.
